Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about Alera Medtech Securement. If you're looking for additional information, please contact us here.

↗ Is Alera Medtech compatible with all forms of PIV catheters?
⌄

Yes, Alera Medtech is designed to be compatible with all standard PIV catheters. The device accommodates various catheter sizes and types commonly used in clinical settings.

↗ Is the Alera Medtech Peripheral IV stabilization device appropriate for all patients with PIVs?
⌄

Alera Medtech is suitable for most patients requiring PIV stabilization. However, individual patient assessment should be conducted to ensure appropriateness based on skin condition and clinical needs.

↗ Any special tips or instructions for fitting the Alera Medtech PIV stabilization device onto the catheter?
⌄

Follow the provided instructions for use. Ensure the catheter is properly inserted and secured before applying the Alera Medtech device. The device should fit snugly without causing discomfort to the patient.

↗ Is Alera Medtech reusable?
⌄

No, Alera Medtech is a single-use device designed for one-time application to maintain sterility and optimal performance.

↗ Do the device and catheter have to be removed together?
⌄

No, the Alera Medtech device can be removed independently from the catheter if needed for assessment or dressing changes.

↗ Is Alera Medtech Latex free?
⌄

Yes, Alera Medtech is latex-free to minimize the risk of allergic reactions in sensitive patients.

↗ If I want to change the tubing, does the device need to be removed?
⌄

No, tubing changes can typically be performed without removing the Alera Medtech device, allowing for continued stabilization during routine care.

↗ Do I have to use a special skin prep with the Alera Medtech stabilization device?
⌄

Follow your facility's standard skin preparation protocols. Alera Medtech is compatible with commonly used skin prep solutions.

↗ Does Alera Medtech have a version for children and infants?
⌄

Please contact us for information about pediatric sizing options and recommendations for use with children and infants.

↗ Can the SureSet device be placed over insertion sites other than the hand?
⌄

Yes, Alera Medtech can be used on various PIV insertion sites including the hand, forearm, and other appropriate locations as determined by clinical assessment.
