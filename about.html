About Us

Alera Medtech:

A Radically Different Medical Device Company

Alera Medtech was created with a clear belief: essential medical devices deserve far more innovation than they have received. Across healthcare, many widely used devices have changed very little over several decades. Clinical practice has moved forward, yet the tools clinicians rely on every day have not kept pace. This creates avoidable complications, unnecessary costs, and limitations in patient care.

Alera exists to overcome these limitations.

We identify under-innovated, high-impact medical devices and redesign them using modern engineering, strong intellectual property, and a commitment to patient access. Our development approach allows rapid and scalable innovation so that improved devices can reach the market faster and more cost effectively than traditional medical technology pathways.

What Makes Alera Different?

Innovation Where It Matters Most

We focus on devices that are clinically essential and used in high volumes, yet have remained largely unchanged for years. By concentrating on overlooked categories rather than emerging trends, Alera creates improvements that benefit large patient populations.

Rapid and Scalable Development

Alera uses streamlined design, engineering, and manufacturing processes that compress development timelines while maintaining quality and regulatory rigor. This model allows us to deliver significant advancements in a fraction.

Intellectual Property as a Strategic Foundation

Most Alera products are protected by strong patents and proprietary design architectures. Intellectual property is central to our strategy and ensures consistent differentiation for clinicians, patients, and partners who rely on our solutions.

Innovation That Remains Affordable

Alera is committed to ensuring that improved performance never results in higher financial burden for patients or healthcare systems. Our engineering priorities support clinical value, operational efficiency, and cost sustainability.

What We Build

Alera focuses on modernizing essential devices in three core clinical categories. Each product family is built with a commitment to safety, usability, and meaningful technological advancement.

Vascular Access

Alera develops advanced securement and infusion solutions designed to reduce complications and improve workflow efficiency.

Our vascular access portfolio includes:

SureSet Securement Systems
featuring SafeTrak Strain Relief Technology for tubing and catheter stability
Dermaflush and Duoflush next-generation saline flush syringes
designed for safety, performance, and ease of use

These solutions help prevent dislodgement and migration while preventing infections, supporting comfort and enhancing clinical efficiency.

Neurology

Cernode range of neurology products support accurate and reliable neurodiagnostic recording and monitoring. Our solutions include:

Hypodermic needle electrodes
EEG electrodes
EMG electrodes
IONM electrodes and accessories

Each device is engineered for consistent signal capture, user-friendly application, and patient comfort.
