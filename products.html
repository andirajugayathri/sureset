Tape is inconsistent.
Sutures are invasive.

Powered by proprietary Safe-Track Strain Relief Technology™, SureSet™ actively manages tubing stress instead of simply holding it in place. The result is controlled stabilization designed to reduce catheter migration, minimize unintended dislodgement, and simplify catheter management across clinical settings.
